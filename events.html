ENIS ROBOT 3.0

Sunday, February 2nd at 8:00 am

After the success of the First and the Second editions of ENISROBOT, Enis Electric Club "CEE" proceeds its honorable performance by manifesting the third edition ENISROBOT 3.0 which will be held on February 02, 2020 at the Sfax International Fair. This competition is dedicated to the tinker heads and robot enthusiasts. The competitors will take part in several challenges, under a common theme, WORLD WAR II, to honor those who fought , those who suffered and those who helped humanity overcome one of the greatest tragedies of all time . This competition will challenge your innovation, patience, hard work, and team spirit. The participants will face three challenges:
-Enigma;
-The great escape;
-The battle of Stalingrad;
Each challenge’s name is inspired by a critical event that shaped the outcome of WW2.
Join us, at ENISROBOT and show the world what you are really made of!

Mechatronic Engineering Discovery

Fondé en 2008, notre club Mechatronic Engineering Discovery est une initiative d'un groupe des élèves ingénieurs motivés. Le club réunit non seulement les étudiants du département de génie mécatronique mais aussi des étudiants de tous les autres départements.
